Software

InfluenceMapper

A large language model-based tool for identifying industry influence in health research.

InfluenceMapper uses large language models to automatically identify relationships to industry — funding, consulting, employment, and other undisclosed ties — in biomedical and public health research. It has been applied to carcinogenic hazard evaluations for substances including benzene, cobalt, and aspartame, and to broader questions of disclosure and conflict-of-interest detection at scale.
